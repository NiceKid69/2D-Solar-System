Planet Descriptions

Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun.

Venus: The second planet, known for its thick atmosphere.

Earth: Our home planet, the only one known to support life.

Mars: The red planet, known for its iron oxide surface.

Jupiter: The largest planet in our solar system.

Saturn: Known for its stunning rings.

Uranus: The tilted planet, unique in its rotation.

Neptune: The farthest planet from the Sun, known for its deep blue color.
