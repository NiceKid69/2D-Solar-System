Planet Descriptions

Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun.

Venus: Known for its thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth: The only planet known to support life.

Mars: The red planet, known for its iron oxide.

Jupiter: The largest planet in our solar system.

Saturn: Famous for its stunning rings.

Uranus: An ice giant with a unique tilt.

Neptune: The farthest planet from the Sun.
